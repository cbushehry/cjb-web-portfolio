Hello, welcome to my online web portfolio. My name is Cameron Bushehry and i'm currently aspiring to become a Full Stack Web Developer. I started on this journey towards the end of 2020 when I realized I wanted to do something that brought me more fulfillment than working at a retail Verizon store selling phones. I recently went through University of Berekeley's Online Coding Bootcamp where I was able to learn the basics of both Front End development and Back End development. Right now I am working full time as a Freight Dispatcher and in my free time working on various projects to expand my knowledge in coding. After moving on from Lifeguarding I also spent time working as a Retail Sales Associate for Victra Verizon. Right now I am spending a lot of my free time reviewing some of the topics I learned in the online Bootcamp so I can get a better understanding of what it takes to develop Full Stack web applications.
